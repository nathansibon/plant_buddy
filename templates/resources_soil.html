Soil Mix Types

Info from guidance by Prof. Bodie V. Pennisi, published by the UofG Ext.

Flowering Houseplants

  • 1x Garden Loam or Potting Soil
  • 1x Sand, Perlite, or Vemiculite
  • 1x Peat Moss

Foliage Plants

3 Options:
  • 1x garden loam or potting soil + 1x sand OR 2x peat moss
  • 1x pine bark + 2x peat moss
  • Equal amounts of sand/pine bark/peat moss
